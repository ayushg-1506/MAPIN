How MAPIN Works

A powerful pipeline to digitize your campus in minutes.

1

Upload Map

Start by uploading a simple static image or PDF of your campus map to the Admin Dashboard. MAPIN instantly converts it into a web-native canvas.

2

Digitize & Pin

Calibrate your map's GPS bounds and drop location pins for Blocks, Labs, and Canteens. Add descriptions and floor numbers for maximum clarity.

3

Navigate

Share your unique campus link. Visitors open it in their mobile browser, search for destinations, and get real-time distance tracking instantly.

Technical Highlights

📍

Live Haversine Distance

MAPIN leverages the browser's Geolocation API and calculates straight-line distances to target pins in real-time using advanced Haversine math.

🗺️

Google Maps Handoff

For large campuses, MAPIN seamlessly passes precise GPS coordinates to the native Google Maps app to provide voice-guided turn-by-turn routing.

📱

Zero-Install PWA

Visitors don't need to download a heavy campus app. MAPIN acts as a Progressive Web App (PWA) with fully responsive UI tailored for mobile screens.

🔌

Offline Capabilities

Engineered for spotty campus networks, MAPIN supports caching map tiles directly into the browser, ensuring visitors never get lost even without LTE.
